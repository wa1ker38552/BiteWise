Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the data coming from?

Most of our data is sourced from the USDA food database. We also obtain additional sources to train the model from other food databases.

Do you store video footage that the app scans?

We do not store or use any of the footage that your app scans after it's been analyzed and shown to you.

Do I need to pay to use the app?

A majority of the features on our app does not require a subscription to use. However, we do offer a premium subscription that unlocks access to more features.
